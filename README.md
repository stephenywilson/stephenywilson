## Featured Project

### [MarketLayer by Catalayer](https://github.com/stephenywilson/MarketLayer)

MarketLayer by Catalayer is an open-source AI market research console for US stocks. It brings Catalayer-style news analysis, signal organization, watchlist research, and AI-assisted decision workflows into a lightweight open-source project.

Designed for research and education only. It does not execute trades or provide financial advice.
